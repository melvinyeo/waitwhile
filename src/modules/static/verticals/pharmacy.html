Pharmacy waitlist 
by Waitwhile

The simplest way to manage your pharmacy customer waitlist. Learn how.

It usually takes half an hour to fill a prescription order. Let customers shop or run other errands while they wait. Simply add their name to your Waitwhile waitlist and just tap a button to alert them with an SMS message when their orders are ready. This will create happier customers that shop more as a result.

For additional insights, you can add custom notes and view waitlist reports to spot trends and identify returning visitors. It's about time to make your customers happier and your business run smoother with Waitwhile!

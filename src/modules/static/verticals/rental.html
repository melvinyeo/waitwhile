Rental service waitlist 
by Waitwhile

The simplest way to manage your rental service waitlist. Learn how.

Whatever cool thing you're renting out at your shop, customers that have to wait at your location on busy days may not always stick around. With Waitwhile, it is easy to schedule waiting customers in a waitlist. Customers can even add themselves and check online how many others are ahead in line.

Once a guest is added they receive a confirmation SMS which you can tailor to your busines. Once their appointment is ready, you can send them an SMS alert with just a tap asking them to return. This means that you don't need to shout names and your gym guests are free to run other errands while waiting for their turn. They'll love you for it!

For additional insights, you can add notes to customers and view waitlist reports to spot trends and identify return visitors. It's about time to make your customers happier and your business run smoother with Waitwhile!
